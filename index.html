Videogames

  • Overwatch

    • Overwatch is a 6v6 teambased hero shooter set on future Earth. The game consists of "heroes" in 3 different classes of tank, support and damage all with different responsibilites. There are currently 32 different playable characters in the game and each has a unique playstyle through the use of abilities such as barrier which blocks damage and different weapons such as massive hammer or a sniper rifle.




    Developer: Blizzard Entertainment (Known for World of Warcraft)




  • Valorant

    • Valorant is a 5v5 teambased tactical shooter where players must either try to plant the spike and wait for it to explode or defend the spike plant sites and defuse it before the timer runs out and it explodes. The game uses an economy system where you earn credits for doing certain objectives such as eliminating an enemy or winning a round. The credits can be used to purchase abilities that are character specific such as a flashbang as well as purchasing better guns to elimnate enemies to secure the objective easier.




    Developer: Riot Games (Known for League of Legends)




  • Dead by Daylight

    • Dead by Daylight is an asymetrical(4v1) horror game where players choose between two different roles. One role is the survivor where you must repair 5 generators across the map in order to power the exit gates to be able to leave. However, survivors must attempt to do this whilst the other role, the killer tries to interrupt them. Killers have special abilities such as high mobility to help chase survivors, one basic attack will make a survivor injured and a second one will put them in the dying state. When in the dying state a killer can carry the survivor to a hook and if individual survivors are hooked 3 times they will be sacrificed and will be removed from the game.




    Developer: Behaviour Interactive
